VPC Sentry

VPC Sentry watches your AWS Virtual Private Cloud environment and notifies you when changes occur. VPC Sentry can:

  • Create a baseline profile of your Cloud environment
  • Perform regular checks of over 50 items to detect change
  • Provide reliable audit logging for forensic research
