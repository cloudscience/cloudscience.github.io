Faq

Simple Questions

Q.

Why do I need Bridge for my OT network?

Many OT devices run software that is unsafe to connect to the Internet, or even a corporate network. Bridge provides a highly secure, monitored, and managed method of enabling that connectivity.

Q.

I don't have a network connection, or WiFi is spotty in my plant, will Bridge work?

Bridge Anchor gateways can utilize many communications channels, including WiFi, LTE/3G Cellular, wired ethernet and Iridium. The appropriate connectivity method will be determined during your integration planning.

Q.

How many OT communications protocols can Anchor support?

Anchor can support most common industrial protocols and interconnects, and Cloud Science will gladly work with you to add support for your protocol if it's not currently available.

Q.

Can I get my data out?

Your data is your data - Cloud Science will enable you to export/download your data if using our cloud storage, or enable you to stream it directly to your own storage in real time.

Q.

How is Bridge priced?

Bridge is priced based on capacity (e.g. number of devices, amount of data thruput), and functionality (e.g. types of bus interconnects, backhaul connectivity, edge computing enablement).

Q.

What separates Bridge from other IoT gateways?

Bridge is a fully managed service, delivering the connectivity, security monitoring and response, and OT integration on an OpEx basis.
